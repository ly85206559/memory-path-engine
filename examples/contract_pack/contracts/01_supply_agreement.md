# Supply Agreement

## Delivery
1 Supplier shall deliver the equipment no later than 10 days after purchase order acceptance.
2 If Supplier misses the delivery deadline, Buyer may issue a written notice and a 5 day cure period applies.
3 If Supplier fails to cure the late delivery within the cure period, Buyer may terminate the affected order and recover direct damages.

## Payment
4 Buyer shall pay undisputed invoices within 30 days after receipt.
5 Buyer may withhold payment for goods that are defective or not delivered.

## Liability
6 Supplier shall indemnify Buyer for third-party claims arising from defective equipment.
