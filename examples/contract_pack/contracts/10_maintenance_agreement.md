# Maintenance Agreement

## Preventive Maintenance
1 Contractor shall perform preventive maintenance once each calendar quarter.
2 Contractor shall provide a maintenance report within 2 business days after each visit.

## Corrective Maintenance
3 Contractor shall begin corrective maintenance within 8 hours after receiving a critical fault notice.
4 If Contractor does not begin corrective maintenance within 8 hours, Customer may engage a third party and offset reasonable costs against fees owed.

## Term
5 Customer may terminate for repeated critical response failures after written notice and a 10 day cure period.
