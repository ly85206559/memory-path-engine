# Data Processing Addendum

## Security
1 Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.
2 Processor shall notify Controller of a confirmed personal data breach without undue delay and no later than 24 hours after confirmation.

## Subprocessors
3 Processor shall not appoint a new subprocessor unless Controller receives prior notice and an opportunity to object.

## Liability
4 Processor shall indemnify Controller for third-party claims arising from Processor's violation of applicable data protection law.
5 Except for breaches of confidentiality or data protection obligations, indirect damages are excluded.
