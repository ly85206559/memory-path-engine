# Support SLA

## Response Times
1 Supplier shall respond to severity one incidents within 30 minutes.
2 Supplier shall resolve severity one incidents within 4 hours unless the delay is caused by Customer's unavailable personnel.

## Service Credits
3 If Supplier misses the resolution target for a severity one incident, Customer receives a service credit equal to 5 percent of monthly support fees.
4 If Supplier misses the resolution target in three separate months within a quarter, Customer may terminate the support order.

## Cooperation
5 Customer shall provide timely access and logs reasonably requested for diagnosis.
