# Distribution Agreement

## Territory
1 Distributor may sell the products only in the assigned territory.
2 Distributor shall not appoint sub-distributors except with Manufacturer's written approval.

## Forecasts
3 Distributor shall provide quarterly rolling forecasts.
4 Manufacturer may reduce supply priority if Distributor repeatedly misses forecast accuracy thresholds.

## Breach
5 Sales outside the territory constitute material breach.
6 Manufacturer may terminate for material breach if Distributor does not cure within 14 days after notice.
