# Logistics Service Agreement

## Pickup and Delivery
1 Carrier shall pick up shipments on the confirmed booking date.
2 Carrier shall deliver domestic shipments within 3 business days unless delay is caused by customs or force majeure.
3 If Carrier fails to deliver on time for reasons within its control, Carrier shall pay a delay credit equal to 2 percent of freight charges.

## Claims
4 Customer must submit cargo damage claims within 10 days after delivery.
5 Carrier is not liable for concealed damage unless Customer documents the damage at delivery.

## Termination
6 Either party may terminate for repeated service failure after 15 days notice and an opportunity to cure.
