# Manufacturing Agreement

## Specifications
1 Manufacturer shall produce goods in accordance with the approved specifications.
2 Buyer may reject goods that materially deviate from specifications.

## Rework
3 Manufacturer shall rework or replace rejected goods at its own cost.
4 If Manufacturer fails to complete rework within 12 days after rejection notice, Buyer may source substitute goods and recover the price difference.

## Warranty
5 Manufacturer warrants the goods for 12 months after delivery.
6 This warranty does not apply to misuse by Buyer.
