# Software License

## License Grant
1 Licensor grants Customer a non-exclusive internal use license for the software.
2 Customer shall not sublicense or transfer the software except with Licensor's prior written consent.

## Audit
3 Licensor may audit Customer's license usage on 15 days written notice.
4 If an audit shows underpayment above 5 percent, Customer shall pay the shortfall and audit costs.

## Termination
5 Licensor may terminate for material breach if Customer fails to cure within 10 days after notice.
6 Upon termination Customer shall cease use and certify deletion of all copies.
