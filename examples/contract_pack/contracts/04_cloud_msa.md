# Cloud Master Services Agreement

## Service Levels
1 Provider shall maintain 99.9 percent monthly uptime for the production service.
2 If monthly uptime falls below 99.9 percent, Customer receives a service credit equal to 10 percent of monthly fees.
3 If uptime falls below 98 percent for two consecutive months, Customer may terminate the affected service without penalty.

## Security
4 Provider shall notify Customer of a confirmed security incident within 24 hours.
5 Provider shall cooperate in remediation and restore service continuity as soon as practicable.

## Fees
6 Customer shall pay monthly fees within 30 days after invoice date.
