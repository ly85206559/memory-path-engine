# Reseller Agreement

## Pricing
1 Reseller shall follow Vendor's approved price list.
2 Reseller shall not offer discounts beyond 8 percent unless Vendor approves in writing.

## Chargebacks
3 If Reseller grants an unauthorized discount, Vendor may charge back the excess amount.
4 Repeated unauthorized discounts constitute material breach.

## Termination
5 Vendor may terminate this agreement for material breach unless Reseller cures within 7 days after notice.
6 Accrued payment obligations survive termination.
