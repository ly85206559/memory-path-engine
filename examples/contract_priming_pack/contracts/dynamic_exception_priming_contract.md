# Dynamic Exception Priming Contract

## Alpha Exceptions
1 Buyer must pay alpha service invoices within 30 days of receipt.
2 Unless alpha service credits are disputed, Buyer must pay alpha service invoices within 30 days of receipt.
3 If alpha service credits are disputed, Buyer may withhold alpha service payment until Seller resolves the dispute.

## Beta Termination
4 When beta platform breach notice is received, review the beta platform breach notice.
5 If beta platform breach remains uncured, Licensor may terminate the beta platform license.
6 After the beta platform license termination, collect beta termination evidence.
7 After beta termination evidence is collected, certify beta platform deletion.
